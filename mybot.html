About this bot

StateBot is powered by a Botpress Table and Knowledge Base. It can answer questions about selected U.S. states, including capital, region, population, GDP, and area.

  • Dataset: 10-12 states with columns - State_ID, State_Name, Capital, Region, Population_Million, GDP_Billion, Area_sqmi.
  • Workflow: Start - Autonomous with Search Knowledge enabled.
  • Try: "Which state has the largest GDP?", "What is the capital of Texas?", "Which region is Florida in?"
